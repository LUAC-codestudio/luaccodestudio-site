ABOUT LUAC CODE STUDIO

LUAC CODE STUDIO is a tech academy, call it a code school. It was started in 2018 by a couple of students in the Lira University Association of Computing (LUAC) after getting fade up of the University IT & Computing curriculum that had barely prepared them to take on the software industry. They embarked on creating an environment for aspiring software engineers and designers to gain professional experience through working on real world projects while collaborating with experienced engineers.
LUAC CODE STUDIO now works with organisations and businesses looking for innovative solutions to their most annoying challenges through running HACKATHONS, BOOTCAMPS & CODE CHALLENGES around specific themes. In doing that, businesses and organisations get solutions to their challenges and aspiring developers get a chance to learn, collaborate with our top engineers while also getting a possible chance to work on a paid project should the organisation wish to push the emerged solution further.
